Instructions:

Use 'W-A-S-D-Q-E' keys to translate the Gasket on the X-axis (A/D), Y-axis(W/S), and Z-axis (Q/E)

Alternatively, use the "Free Translate" button to move the Gasket with your mouse. Press the button again to lock to gasket.

To rotate the gasket, click a rotate mode and use the mouse to rotate the gasket on that axis. Click the rotate mode again to disable rotation on that axis. .
